Who Are We?

It all started back in 1996 when the team was relaxing in their Mountain View apartment trying to figure out if they were able to drive to a bar, or if they were just a little too TPSY. It was a classic problem that needed solving. If only they had their iPhone 6's back in the 90's...

Fast-forward to 2015 and these gentlemen have developed the world's first 2-in-1 Blood Alcohol Calculator and Bar Locator application available on any device. These guys not only solved their original problem, but have created a modern solution that promotes the safety of mankind, thus making the world a better place!
